Free Online Random Decision Tools

Decision Hub provides a variety of free tools to help you make choices instantly. Whether you need to flip a coin in 3D, roll custom dice, or draw lots, we have the perfect solution for your decision fatigue.

  • Magic Lamp: Ask a Yes or No question and get witty advice from the genie.
  • 3D Coin Flip: A realistic coin toss with physics-based results for heads or tails.
  • Dice of Destiny: Roll custom dice for lunch menus, board games, or any random selection.
  • Ladder Climb: The classic "Ghost Leg" game for fair betting and role assignment with friends.
  • Random Team Maker: Quickly divide a list of people into fair groups or teams.
  • Card Flip & Drawing Lots: Simple and fast ways to pick winners or determine order.
  • Time Bomb Game: A high-tension wire-cutting game for fun group decisions.

Overcome Hamlet Syndrome and stop worrying about small choices. Our tools use the Web Crypto API to ensure 100% fairness and cryptographic randomness.
